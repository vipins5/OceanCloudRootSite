01 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide website owners with information about how visitors interact with their site.

Cookies do not contain personal information such as your name or payment details. They are not programs and cannot carry viruses or malware.

02 How We Use Cookies

OceanCloud's website (oceancloudconsults.com) is a static website. We currently set no tracking or analytics cookies on your device without your consent. The site operates without server-side sessions or login state.

When we activate website analytics (Google Analytics 4), it will only load after you explicitly accept cookies via our consent banner. Until then, the site places no cookies on your browser.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies to build profiles for targeted advertising, and we do not sell data to third-party advertisers.

03 Types of Cookies We Use

Currently Active Cookies

This website currently sets no first-party cookies on your device.

Analytics Cookies (pending — only set after you accept)

Once Google Analytics 4 is enabled and you have given consent, the following cookies will be set:

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
_ga Analytics Google Analytics 4 — distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics 4 — distinguishes sessions within 24 hours 24 hours
_ga_XXXXXXXX Analytics Google Analytics 4 — maintains session state for this property 2 years

Local Storage (not a cookie)

We store your cookie preference (oc_cookie_consent) in your browser's local storage — not as a cookie. This is a simple "accepted" or "declined" value used only to remember your choice so we do not prompt you on every visit. It contains no personal data and is never sent to our servers.

04 Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services. Some connect to external servers when you use the site:

  • Google Fonts — web font delivery (Google LLC, US). Your browser requests font files from Google's servers when loading pages. Google may log your IP address server-side but does not set tracking cookies via this mechanism. Google Fonts Privacy FAQ
  • EmailJS — contact form delivery (EmailJS Ltd). Used only when you submit the contact form. Your form data is transmitted to EmailJS servers to deliver the email. No persistent cookies are set in your browser. EmailJS Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI — powers our website chatbot. When you send a message via the chat widget, your message is transmitted to Google's Generative AI API to generate a response. No cookies are set and queries are not linked to your identity. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics 4 (Measurement ID: G-2ZVXYXDHEZ) — website traffic analysis (Google LLC, US). Only loaded after you accept cookies. Data is processed under Google's standard contractual clauses with IP anonymisation enabled. Google Privacy Policy

These third parties operate under their own privacy policies. OceanCloud is not responsible for their data practices.

05 Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Cookie Consent Banner

When Google Analytics 4 is activated, a consent banner will appear on your first visit. You can accept or decline analytics cookies. Your choice is saved in browser local storage and respected on all subsequent visits. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing local storage for this site in your browser's developer tools.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View and delete cookies stored for a specific site
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all sites
  • Clear all cookies automatically when you close the browser

Instructions for common browsers: Chrome · Firefox · Safari · Edge

This website currently sets no cookies on your device. Any analytics features will require your explicit consent before activation.
