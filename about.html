Our Story

A Decade of Microsoft Excellence

OceanCloud was born in 2013 when our founders — former Microsoft engineers and enterprise architects — recognised that most organisations were barely scratching the surface of what SharePoint and Office 365 could do for them.

Starting with a handful of UK financial services clients, we grew our practice steadily, earning a reputation for honest assessments, clean delivery, and genuine post-project support. Today, OceanCloud is a recognised Microsoft Solutions Partner with over 150 completed projects across the UK, Ireland, and Europe.

We remain independent, privately held, and laser-focused on the Microsoft ecosystem — which means our consultants go deep, not broad, and our clients get advice untainted by vendor incentives.
