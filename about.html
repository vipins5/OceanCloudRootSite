Our Story

A Decade of Microsoft Excellence

OceanCloud was born in 2013 when our founders — former Microsoft engineers and enterprise architects — recognised that most organisations were barely scratching the surface of what SharePoint and Office 365 could do for them.

Starting with a focused practice in enterprise Microsoft 365 deployments, we built a reputation for honest assessments, clean delivery, and genuine post-project support. Today, OceanCloud is a recognised Microsoft Solutions Partner with over 150 completed projects across the United States and internationally.

We remain independent, privately held, and laser-focused on the Microsoft ecosystem — which means our consultants go deep, not broad, and our clients get advice untainted by vendor incentives.

Our Journey

Twelve Years of Microsoft Excellence

2013

Founded

OceanCloud established by former Microsoft engineers and enterprise architects, with a mission to unlock the full value of SharePoint for organisations.

2015

Microsoft Gold Partner

Achieved Microsoft Gold Partner status in Collaboration and Content — recognising 25+ successful deployments and our first batch of 10 certified consultants.

2018

Power Platform Practice Launched

Expanded into Power Apps, Power Automate, and Power BI as Microsoft shifted its low-code strategy — now one of our highest-growth service lines.

2020

Teams Surge & Remote Work

Delivered emergency Teams rollouts for 12 clients during the pandemic, enabling thousands of workers to collaborate securely from day one of lockdown.

2022

Microsoft Solutions Partner Designation

Achieved the new Microsoft Solutions Partner status across Modern Work and Security — replacing Gold Partner as Microsoft's highest partner tier.

2024

M365 Copilot Practice Launched

Built a dedicated Copilot readiness and deployment practice — helping organisations safely roll out Microsoft 365 Copilot with proper governance and measurable ROI.

2026

150+ Projects & Growing

Over 150 completed engagements, 40+ active Microsoft certifications, and a team of specialists serving clients across the United States and internationally.
