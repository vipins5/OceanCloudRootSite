How We Work

Four steps to a transformed workplace.

01

Discover

We deep-dive into your current environment — auditing your M365 tenant, interviewing stakeholders, mapping workflows, and benchmarking your Microsoft Secure Score. No assumptions, no templates forced onto your situation.

02

Design

Our architects produce a solution blueprint: information architecture, governance model, security posture, integration map, and a phased roadmap — reviewed and approved by you before a single line of code is written.

03

Deploy

We build and migrate iteratively, keeping your live environment intact throughout. Daily standups, shared project tracking, and milestone sign-offs keep you in control. Cutover is meticulously tested — zero surprises on go-live day.

04

Adopt

Technology only delivers value when people use it. We run bespoke training, champions programmes, and adoption KPI dashboards to embed the solution in your culture — then stay for 12 months of hypercare to ensure it lasts.

Common Questions

SharePoint & M365 FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from prospective clients.

How much does SharePoint consulting cost?

Our fixed-price engagements cover everything from focused advisory retainers ($2,200/month) to full intranet builds and migrations. Typical project ranges: SharePoint intranet from $7,500 (small org) to $55,000+ (enterprise); M365 migration from $18–$30/user; Power Platform apps from $4,500. Every project starts with a free 60-minute scoping call — you'll receive a written estimate within 24 hours. Most clients see measurable ROI within 3–6 months.

How long does a Microsoft 365 migration take?

A typical M365 migration for 100–500 users takes 4–8 weeks, covering discovery, pilot migration, cutover, and post-migration support. Larger organisations (500–5,000+ users) with complex on-premises environments typically require 10–16 weeks. We use industry-leading migration tooling to ensure zero downtime and zero data loss.

Do you offer ongoing SharePoint support after a project ends?

Yes — all clients have the option to move onto a SharePoint support retainer after project delivery. Our support plans include a dedicated consultant, guaranteed SLA response times, monthly health checks, and proactive monitoring of your M365 tenant. Support is available Monday–Friday 9am–6pm EST, with emergency response available 24/7 on premium plans.

Can you rebuild our existing SharePoint intranet without losing data?

Absolutely. We specialise in SharePoint intranet modernisation — migrating content, permissions, and metadata from legacy SharePoint 2010/2013/2016 or classic SharePoint Online sites to a fully modern SharePoint Online experience. Your data is migrated in parallel so the old intranet stays live until the new one is signed off.

What is a Microsoft Solutions Partner and why does it matter?

Microsoft Solutions Partner is Microsoft's highest partner designation, replacing the old "Gold Partner" tier. It requires verified customer success metrics, certified employees (we hold 40+ active certifications), and a proven track record of successful deployments. When you work with a Solutions Partner like OceanCloud, you get access to dedicated Microsoft support escalations, early feature previews, and the assurance that your consultant is regularly validated by Microsoft itself.

Do you work with small businesses, or only large enterprises?

We work with organisations of all sizes — from 20-person professional services firms to 5,000-seat multinational enterprises. Our service tiers are designed to scale: smaller clients benefit from our fixed-price intranet packages and SharePoint Quickstart offering, while enterprise clients get fully bespoke programmes with dedicated project management.

What is your approach to SharePoint security and compliance?

We implement a Zero Trust security model by default across all engagements — combining Entra ID, Conditional Access policies, Microsoft Purview sensitivity labels, and Defender for Microsoft 365. For regulated industries (healthcare, legal, finance), we layer on sector-specific compliance controls and can support HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR requirements.

Can you help us roll out Microsoft 365 Copilot safely?

Yes — Copilot readiness and deployment is one of our fastest-growing service areas. The biggest risk most organisations face before enabling Copilot is oversharing — documents and data that are technically accessible to everyone but were never meant to be. We run a Copilot Readiness Assessment covering data hygiene, sensitivity labelling, and governance posture, then manage the structured rollout, build prompt libraries, configure acceptable-use policies, and measure ROI through Viva Insights dashboards. The result: Copilot that delivers real productivity gains without exposing sensitive data.

What industries do you specialise in?

We have deep experience across healthcare (HIPAA-compliant M365, clinical Teams), legal (matter-centric SharePoint, eDiscovery, DLP), financial services (SOC 2, Purview, regulatory Power BI), public sector (GCC cloud, governance frameworks), retail (frontline worker enablement, Teams Shifts), and manufacturing (shop floor Teams, operational dashboards). That said, our core methodology — discover, design, deploy, adopt — works for any organisation that runs on Microsoft 365.
