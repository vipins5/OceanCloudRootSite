FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a SharePoint intranet take to build?

A focused intranet commonly takes eight to twelve weeks. Content readiness, number of departments, integrations, and governance decisions affect the timeline.

Can you migrate content from an existing intranet?

Yes. We inventory and classify existing content, assign owners, archive low-value material, and migrate approved content into the new structure.

Will the intranet work inside Microsoft Teams?

Yes. Viva Connections can bring SharePoint news, navigation, dashboard cards, and resources into Teams for desktop and mobile users.
